The power of a baby is that
her 7 year old brother when
in crisis because he needs to
stop playing legos and get
ready for bed turns the corner
in a pout - feet stomping, brow
furrowed, arms crossed,
sees his little sister, pauses -
puts his hand gently on her
back, looks her in the eye,
smiling, kisses her cheek,
then resumes his tantrum.
